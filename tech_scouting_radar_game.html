Tech Scout Radar Challenge

A fast classroom game where teams act as Technology Managers. Read each technology signal, assess its impact and risk, then place it in the correct radar ring: Adopt, Trial, Assess, or Hold.

Technology Intelligence Technology Scouting Technology Radar TM Decision-Making

Game Setup

Recommended for a 10–15 minute activity. Each group may use one device, then compare scores at the end.

How to Win

1. Read the technology signal carefully.

2. Decide where it belongs in the radar: Adopt, Trial, Assess, or Hold.

3. Add a short rationale. Bonus points are given when you mention business impact, risk, customer value, or strategic fit.

4. Highest score wins. In case of tie, the faster team wins.
