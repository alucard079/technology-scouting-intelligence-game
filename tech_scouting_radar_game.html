Tech Scout Radar Challenge

A classroom game where teams act as Technology Managers. Read each technology signal, assess its impact and risk, then place it in the correct radar ring: Adopt, Trial, Assess, or Hold.

Technology Intelligence Technology Scouting Technology Radar

Game Setup

Recommended for a 10–15 minute activity. Each group uses one device and screenshots the final score.

How to Win

1. Read each technology signal carefully.

2. Choose Adopt, Trial, Assess, or Hold.

3. Write a short rationale using impact, risk, maturity, customer value, or strategic fit.

4. You may skip and return to signals as long as there is time.

5. Screenshot the final score. Highest score wins.
